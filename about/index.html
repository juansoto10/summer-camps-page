About

We are a group of students from the University of Cauca in Colombia who are pursuing degrees in foreign languages. As part of our thesis work, we conducted interviews with graduates of our program who have attended summer camps in the USA as foreign language students. Through these conversations, we gained valuable insights into the unique and unforgettable experiences that await students at these camps.

Our goal with this site is to provide a comprehensive guide for new students who are interested in this exciting opportunity, as well as to share the personal accounts of our interviewees. We believe that summer camps in the USA offer a unique and immersive way to enhance language skills, learn about American culture, and make lifelong connections.

Our team is made up of individuals from a variety of backgrounds and with a range of experiences, but we all share a passion for language and a love of travel. We are excited to share our project with you and hope that it inspires you to embark on your own language learning journey.

Thank you for visiting our site. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to reach out to us through our Contact page.
