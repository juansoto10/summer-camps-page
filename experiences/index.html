Experiences

Dive into the world of summer camps in the USA with this collection of stories from foreign language students who have embarked on this exciting adventure. From cultural immersion to personal growth, these stories showcase the transformative power of a summer camp experience.

In this section we present the entire experiences classified under the three categories that are the communicative competencies linguistic, pragmatic and sociolinguistic. Each category includes the experiences that have elements related to its corresponding competency. They were carefully selected based on the definitions and information found in the frame of references about each communicative competence.

Sociolinguistic Experiences

The 36 experiences below are the ones that present sociolinguistic competences such as cultural shock, cultural differences, effective comunication amog others.

Linguistic Experiences

The following 14 experiences were classified under the linguistic competence, due to the linguistic elements found in each one of them, such as the difficulties to speak or understand English because of lack of vocabulary, fear to make mistakes, fluency, speech disorders, among others.

Pragmatic Experiences

The next 25 experiences are the ones that present pragmatic elements including paralinguistics, proxemics, non verbal communication among others.
